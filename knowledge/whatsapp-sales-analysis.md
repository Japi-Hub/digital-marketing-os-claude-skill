# WhatsApp and DM Sales Analysis

The goal is to diagnose how conversations move or fail to move toward a commercial decision.

## What to inspect

- First response
- Qualification questions
- Timing of price disclosure
- Objections
- CTA clarity
- Follow-up quality
- Message length
- Tone
- Whether the seller guides or only responds
- Whether the next step is obvious

## Common failure points

### Lead cools down after price
Possible causes:
- price sent without context
- value not established
- no next step
- weak objection handling

### Lead asks many questions but does not buy
Possible causes:
- seller is acting like a catalog
- no diagnosis
- no recommendation
- no decision path

### Lead disappears after receiving information
Possible causes:
- message too long
- unclear CTA
- no urgency
- no reason to continue

### Many interested leads but few sales
Possible causes:
- poor qualification
- slow response
- offer mismatch
- weak follow-up
- wrong traffic source

## Output format

For each conversation or group of conversations, produce:
1. Observed pattern
2. Where the lead cooled down
3. Likely cause
4. Recommended fix
5. Sales script improvement
6. Content opportunity
7. Ad or landing page implication

## Content opportunities from conversations

Turn repeated questions and objections into:
- reels
- carousels
- FAQs
- email sequences
- WhatsApp follow-up messages
- remarketing ads
- landing page sections
