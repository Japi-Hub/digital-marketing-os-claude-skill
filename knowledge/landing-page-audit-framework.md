# Landing Page Audit Framework

A landing page must make the commercial decision easier.

## First 5 seconds

Check whether the visitor can immediately understand:
- what this is
- who it is for
- what problem it solves
- what result it helps create
- what to do next

## Sections to evaluate

1. Hero section
2. Offer explanation
3. Problem framing
4. Benefits
5. Proof or credibility
6. Process or how it works
7. Objections
8. Pricing or value framing
9. FAQ
10. CTA
11. Form or checkout

## Common problems

- The page explains but does not sell
- The CTA appears before value is clear
- The offer is buried
- The audience is not specific
- The proof is weak
- There are too many sections with no hierarchy
- The copy is clever but unclear
- The page does not answer price, trust, timing, or suitability objections

## Audit output

1. Main diagnosis
2. Conversion bottleneck
3. What to keep
4. What to change
5. Missing sections
6. Copy improvements
7. CTA improvements
8. SEO/AEO opportunities
9. Content that can be derived from the page
10. Priority action plan

## AEO angle

Structure content so AI systems and users can clearly identify:
- who the offer helps
- what problem it solves
- what makes it different
- common questions
- use cases
- proof points
- next steps
