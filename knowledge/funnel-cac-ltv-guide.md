# Funnel, CAC and LTV Guide

Marketing performance must be evaluated across the full funnel, not only inside the ads platform.

## Funnel stages

1. Impression
2. Click or engagement
3. Landing page visit
4. Form submission or WhatsApp/DM start
5. Qualification
6. Follow-up
7. Sales conversation
8. Purchase or booking
9. Repeat purchase or retention

## Core metrics

- CTR: clicks / impressions
- Landing conversion rate: leads / visits
- WhatsApp conversion rate: qualified conversations / conversations started
- Close rate: customers / qualified leads
- CAC: marketing spend / customers
- LTV: average revenue per customer over time
- Payback: CAC / monthly gross profit per customer

## Diagnosis logic

### Ads problem
Likely when:
- CTR is low
- CPC is high
- CPM is high without quality traffic
- Comments/reactions show mismatch
- Creative does not communicate the offer clearly

### Landing problem
Likely when:
- CTR is acceptable but form/lead rate is low
- Users click but do not continue
- CTA is unclear
- Offer is not understood quickly
- Objections are not answered

### Lead quality problem
Likely when:
- CPL is low but close rate is poor
- Leads ask irrelevant questions
- Leads cannot afford the offer
- Leads do not match the desired customer profile

### Sales/WhatsApp problem
Likely when:
- Leads are qualified but do not buy
- Response time is slow
- Follow-up is weak
- Price is sent too early
- No clear next step exists

### Offer problem
Likely when:
- Traffic and landing are acceptable but conversion remains weak
- People understand the offer but do not see urgency or value
- Repeated objections focus on price, trust, relevance, or timing

## Output rule

Always identify the most likely bottleneck and the next test that would prove or disprove it.
